## 1. AI Chatbots That Actually Understand What People Say (Thanks to NLP)

Most chatbots are dumb.  

They only reply if you say the exact phrase they expect. It’s like talking to a wall.

**NLP (Natural Language Processing)** changes that. It’s like teaching your chatbot how to understand **real human talk**—slang, typos, emotions, even sarcasm.

So when someone messages, *“hey do u guys do laser teeth stuff?”*—a normal bot gets confused.  

An **NLP chatbot** gets it: *Oh, they mean laser teeth whitening. Here’s info + a booking link.*
