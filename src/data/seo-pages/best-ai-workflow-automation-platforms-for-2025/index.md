## What Is an AI Workflow?

An AI workflow is a sequence of automated steps powered by intelligence that connects processes, handles repetitive tasks, and ensures nothing slips through the cracks.

```  
Customer messages ➜ AI chatbot responds ➜ Inquiry logged ➜ Follow-up triggered ➜ Reminder/review request sent automatically  
```

It’s more than automation—it’s workflow intelligence tailored to your business.
