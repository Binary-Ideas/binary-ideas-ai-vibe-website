Glossary Section 

CRM (Customer Relationship Management):
 A software system that helps businesses track customer info, conversations, and sales in one place. Makes follow-ups and customer care way easier.

Natural Language Processing (NLP):
 AI’s ability to understand, read, and respond to human language. It’s what lets chatbots “get” what you type and answer in a natural way.

HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act):
 A U.S. law that protects health data. If your business deals with patient info, your AI platform must meet HIPAA rules to stay compliant.

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation):
 A European privacy law that requires businesses to handle personal data safely. Even local companies in Lorton may deal with GDPR if they serve EU clients.
